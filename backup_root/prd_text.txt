Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Product
Personal Portfolio Website for Rishabh Mishra
Product Vision
Build a modern, high‑performance developer portfolio that presents Rishabh Mishra as an AI‑Augmented
Full‑Stack Developer  capable of building scalable systems, participating in hackathons, and delivering
freelance projects. The portfolio should impress recruiters and potential clients within the first few seconds
and clearly communicate technical capability, project depth, and developer workflow.
1. Goals
Primary Goals - Impress recruiters within the first 5 seconds - Increase chances of internship opportunities -
Attract  freelance  project  inquiries  -  Showcase  real  engineering  work  and  architecture  thinking  -
Demonstrate AI‑augmented development workflow
Secondary Goals - Showcase hackathon participation - Display GitHub activity - Provide easy resume access -
Allow visitors to ask questions through an AI chatbot
2. Target Audience
Primary - Tech recruiters - Startup founders - Engineering managers
Secondary - Developer community - Hackathon judges - Potential collaborators
